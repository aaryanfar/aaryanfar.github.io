Brief Bio

I am Assistant Professor of Mechanical Engineering at American University of Beirut (AUB) as well as Visiting Researcher at California Institute of Technology (Caltech). My research mainly is on developing devices and multiphysics methods for energy related applications, including designing state-of-the-art high energy, safe and self-sustaining rechargeable batteries and predicting the corrosion/fracture of materials in extreme states. Here is more on my Background, Research, Publications and Teaching experiences as well as my Resume/CV.
